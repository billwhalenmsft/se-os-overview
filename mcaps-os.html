The problem — name it so we don't rebuild it

Every persona translates their job into tool taxonomy.

An AE, an SE, a CSAM each live in a sprawl — MSX/CRM, MCEM guidance, decks, demo envs, success plans, a dozen portals. They must translate what they're trying to do into our tool menu. And the value leaks at the seams between them.

🧩

Tool sprawl

Organized around tools, not the job. The human does the translating the system should do.

💧

Deals leak at the seams

ATU→STU (is it qualified + designed?) and STU→CSU (did the win deploy + consume?) are lossy email threads, not tracked events.

🔁

Nothing compounds

Every engagement is a one-off. The best seller's moves don't propagate. Customer signals evaporate after the meeting.

The vision

The system holds the persona model — so the human just does the job.

MCAPS OS knows who you are, what you're aligned to, and what you're trying to do — and curates one coherent experience around it — for every persona on the deal team, across the whole MCEM lifecycle.

It inverts the sprawl: the right surfaces, the right intelligence, and the next‑best‑actions come to the persona. And the handoffs between personas become in‑product events on one shared spine — so nothing drops at the seams.

Who it wraps — the rooms of the house

ATU, STU, CSU — every role, including the manager altitude.

Managers don't work a single deal — they see the rollup across their team. MCAPS OS curates a portfolio view for them, not a deal view.

How we build it today already

This is assembly, not R&D. The parts exist.

The honest pitch: the brains, the data, the spine, the shell, and the engine are already here. The risk is assembly + adoption — not invention.

Persona brains
Installed MCAPS‑IQ role agents map 1:1 — role-ae/role-ats (ATU), role-specialist/role-se (STU), role-csa/role-csam (CSU), plus mcem-flow.EXISTS
Data spine
Live MSX/CRM (opportunities, milestones, deal teams, territories) + M365/WorkIQ + the signal store. Alignment is read from real CRM.EXISTS
The shell
One front door + four job‑modes composed from surface-placement.json — adding a persona dimension is a manifest edit, not a rebuild.EXISTS
The build engine
The recipe runner (use case → recipe → build → seed → run → package) is the seller's Compose mode, with tokenomics baked in.EXISTS
First agent shipped
Meeting Miner mines new opportunities from existing meeting intelligence and stages them in CRM, approval‑gated. Tested on Pentair.SHIPPED

The one honest gap: a persona profile + the routing that binds role → agents → MCEM → curated surfaces. That's the next build — and it's on top of everything above.
